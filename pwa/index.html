Settings

Identity

Peer ID -

Generate a new identity and notify your contacts. Useful after a suspected compromise. Your contacts keep their chat history; the old identity works for 7 more days so in-transit messages still arrive.

Encryption

Encrypt all local data with a passphrase. Without this, anyone with access to your device can read your messages and keys.

✓ Encryption is active

Data Backup

Export your data as an encrypted file. Use this to move to a new device or restore after reinstalling.

Decoy Mode

When enabled, the app appears as a calculator. Enter your unlock code on the keypad to reveal messages.

Unlock Code

Duress passphrase

Set a separate passphrase that silently wipes all data when typed at unlock. Use only under coercion — the app will appear to unlock normally but will immediately destroy every message, contact, and key.

Notifications

Enable push notifications to receive messages when the app is in the background.

Emergency

Instantly destroy all keys, messages, and session data. This cannot be undone.

WebRTC Privacy

Relay-only mode

When enabled, WebRTC connections route through TURN relays only. Your IP address is never exposed to peers. Disabling this may improve connection speed but reveals your IP.

Cover traffic

Emits a small encrypted message roughly every half-second to hide your real activity. Uses about 8 KB/s of data when idle.
